CoDecode

When I was seven years old, I wanted to be a spy. To encourage, or rather flatter, this fantasy, my parents purchased a beginner's "spy kit" for me, part of which was a small handbook on various ciphers and codes used to hide the meanings of messages. CoDecode is my adult realization of that, with cryptography and its potential in the modern era of computers being a high area of interest, one that stuck around for much longer than the fake mustache. Regularly updated as I complete more complex coding algorithms.
